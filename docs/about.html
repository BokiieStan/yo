About UTAU Kevin

Kevin V2 is an advanced voicebank for UTAU software, featuring more samples and better recordings for unparalleled vocal expression and emotional depth. This enhanced version offers smoother transitions, precise pitch control, and a wide range of styles to bring your musical visions to life with authenticity and nuance.

Kevin's vocal styles include: Sweet V2, Soft V2, V2 (Normal), Dark V2, Radiant V1, Solid V2, Power V1, Soft V1, and the V1 versions of the V2 styles for versatile performances across genres.
