About UTAU Kevin

The UTAU exclusive singing voice database "UTAU Kevin" is a powerful and versatile voice database designed for professional music production.
It features multiple vocal styles and expressive capabilities. His clear, dynamic singing voice is both powerful and nuanced, and can handle any genre.

UTAU Kevin is now available as a V2 voicebank, incorporating cutting-edge synthesis advancements for unparalleled realism and emotional depth. This updated version enhances pitch accuracy, adds subtle vocal inflections, and provides smoother transitions between notes, allowing for more fluid and expressive performances. Whether crafting heartfelt ballads or energetic anthems, Kevin V2 adapts seamlessly, bringing characters to life with authentic vocal textures and dynamic range that captivate listeners.

Explore the layers of Kevin's voice: from soft whispers to powerful crescendos, each vocal style is meticulously crafted to flow naturally, adding depth and dimension to your compositions. With V2's improved algorithms, experience the evolution of UTAU technology that empowers creators to push the boundaries of musical storytelling.
